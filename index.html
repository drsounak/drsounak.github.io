About

"Modelling the mathematics of cancer, teaching the next generation of engineers."

From ETL pipelines at HP, to traffic-prediction AI at IIT Kharagpur, to pioneering multi-scale tumour growth models for my PhD at BHU — and applied predictive ML at PwC. My path has always led back to research and education.

Today I teach AI, Machine Learning, and Soft Computing while pursuing research at the intersection of mathematical biology and data science.
